How to use

Orientation & required measurements

Tap tones tell you how stiff or flexible a plate is; the FFT shows where the main resonances sit so you can compare tops, backs, and boxes.

How to use

  1. Place a mic 3–6 inches from the plate/guitar.
  2. Tap cleanly near center or edge based on what you want to hear.
  3. Record a short tap (or load a WAV).
  4. Zoom the waveform to focus; the FFT updates to that window.
  5. Read note + cents for each peak.

Accuracy tips

  • Tap firmly without clipping the mic.
  • Re-tap if peaks look smeared; move slightly.
  • Quieter rooms reduce false low peaks.
